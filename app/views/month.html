People can apply for up to 3 courses in their initial application. If these are all unsuccessful, the applicant can apply again an unlimited number of times, but for only a single course at a time.

Applicants are only counted once. If they have applied again, they are not counted in the first column, and only their most recent status is counted in the second column.

If an applicant applied to multiple courses, they are counted under the highest row which applies.

For example, if they have received 1 offer, declined 1 offer, and are awaiting a provider decision on a third course, they are counted under “Received an offer”.
